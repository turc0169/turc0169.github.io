About Me

I grew up in Curran Ontario on a back concession road I'm sure still only has dial-up available to them. Being very isolated and without opportunity I moved out early and began my journey in independence. That hit like a brick wall and I survived the first two years living in Alexandria, Ontario. After that I came back to Ottawa, where I was born and am proud to call home. I am very driven and with my diverse skillset I am looking for more experiences and am excited to create more. I'm looking forward to hearing from you and about what we'll be creating in the future.

Thank you for visiting my website and be sure to check back soon as I am always adding content.
